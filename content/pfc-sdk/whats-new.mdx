# What's New & Changelog

Perfectly Clear v10 launches our first AI-based image correction technology - called **Perfectly Clear AI**. This amazing new correction technology pairs our classic deep science with AI-based corrections and AI Preset Selection to provide the most robust fully automatic image correction available today.
